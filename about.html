Who We Are

Mr. John J. Junkard, Sr., our founder, purchased a small factory in Erie, Pennsylvania, USA in the late spring of 1995. Since then, he hired a small team and began manufacturing and designing custom made T-shirts in all different shapes and sizes.

Since then, our team has carried on his tradition in making high-quality, fully-designed T-shirts at a very LOW price.


The factory in Erie, PA

What We Do

While our company has grown and sold over 200 million uniquely-designed T-shirts worldwide in the last 24 years (and counting), we have evolved our business model to listen to YOU, the customer, to provide us YOUR unique design to print our our high-quality 100% Egyptian-style hybrid polyester-cotton shirts at an affordable price.

Our mission is to empower all of our customers to freely express his or her views to the world with our T-shirts and wear them with pride!

Disclaimer: We do NOT accept any designs that are considered offensive or controversial.
